What is Khayyamiyya?

(خيامية) Khayyamiyya is an ancient Egyptian art form of appliqué tent-making that dates back centuries. The name derives from the Arabic word "khayma" (خيمة), meaning tent.

This intricate craft involves cutting colorful cotton fabrics into geometric and floral patterns, then hand-stitching them onto a background cloth to create stunning, vibrant tapestries. Each piece is a masterwork of precision, patience, and cultural heritage.
